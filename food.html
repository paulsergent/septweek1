Meat

Meat is an animal type of food.

Meat is a part of an animal destined to be consumed.

Pork
Pork is the meat of pigs
Beef
Beef is the meat of cows
Mutton
Mutton is the meat of Sheep
Poultry
Poultry is the meat from chickens, ducks and Turkeys
Venison
Venison is the meat from deer and any animal that has been hunted
